<p><p>Surgical smoke evacuators are essential devices used in medical settings to remove harmful smoke generated during surgical procedures. The market can be divided into two main types: portable smoke evacuators and stationary smoke evacuators. Portable smoke evacuators are lightweight and mobile, allowing easy maneuverability around surgical environments, ideal for use in various operating rooms. In contrast, stationary smoke evacuators are fixed units, providing continuous suction in high-demand areas, ensuring consistent smoke removal during extensive procedures. Both types enhance safety by minimizing exposure to surgical smoke.</p></p>
