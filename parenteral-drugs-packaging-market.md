<p><strong>The Parenteral Drugs Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC)</li><li>Polyolefin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parenteral drugs packaging includes various materials, notably Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) and polyolefin. PVC is widely used for its flexibility and barrier properties, making it suitable for IV bags and medical tubing. However, concerns about plasticizers in PVC have led to a shift toward polyolefin. Polyolefin materials, such as polyethylene and polypropylene, offer excellent chemical resistance and are free from harmful additives, making them safer for pharmaceutical applications. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the sterility and stability of parenteral drugs.</p></p>
