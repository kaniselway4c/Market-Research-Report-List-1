<p><p>The liquid crystal panel display market is primarily categorized into three main types: TN (Twisted Nematic) displays, VA (Vertical Alignment) displays, and IPS (In-Plane Switching) displays. TN displays are known for fast response times and are commonly used in gaming, but have limited viewing angles and color reproduction. VA displays offer better contrast and deeper blacks, making them suitable for multimedia. IPS displays provide superior color accuracy and wide viewing angles, ideal for professional and design work. Other market segments may include technologies like OLED and Mini-LED.</p></p>
