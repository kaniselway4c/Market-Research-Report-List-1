<p><p>The automotive cabin air temperature sensor market is divided into several types: thermistor type, thermocouple type, and others. </p><p>Thermistor type sensors, known for their precision, use resistance changes in response to temperature variations, making them ideal for cabin climate control. </p><p>Thermocouple type sensors operate on the principle of voltage generation through temperature differences, suitable for a broad range of applications. </p><p>Other sensors may include infrared or semiconductor-based options, offering varied features to enhance cabin comfort and efficiency.</p></p>
