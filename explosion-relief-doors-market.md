<p><p>The Explosion Relief Doors market includes two primary types: bottom hinged and top hinged explosion relief doors. Bottom hinged explosion relief doors open outward from the bottom, allowing pressure to relieve efficiently while maintaining structural integrity. In contrast, top hinged explosion relief doors swing open from the top, facilitating rapid evacuation of explosive gases and debris. Both designs are essential in industries like chemical processing and manufacturing, ensuring safety by preventing pressure buildup and minimizing damage during explosive events.</p></p>
