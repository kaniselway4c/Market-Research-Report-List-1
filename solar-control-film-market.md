<p><p>The solar control film market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Clear (Non-Reflective)** films offer transparency while reducing UV rays and heat, ideal for maintaining natural light.</p><p>2. **Dyed (Non-Reflective)** films incorporate color to reduce glare and solar heat gain without a reflective surface, providing aesthetic and functional benefits.</p><p>3. **Vacuum Coated (Reflective)** films utilize a metallic layer to reflect solar radiation, significantly enhancing heat rejection while providing a mirrored finish for privacy and aesthetics. Each type caters to unique needs in energy efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
