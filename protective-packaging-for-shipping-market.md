<p><ul><li>Active Systems</li><li>Passive Systems</li><li>Hybrid Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protective packaging for shipping can be categorized into three main types: active, passive, and hybrid systems. Active systems actively respond to environmental changes, utilizing technologies like temperature control or moisture regulation to protect products. Passive systems rely on materials and designs to absorb shocks and prevent damage without additional mechanisms, such as bubble wrap or foam. Hybrid systems combine features of both active and passive solutions, offering enhanced protection through integrated technologies while maintaining the simplicity of passive materials.</p></p>
