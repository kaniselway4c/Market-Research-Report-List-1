<p><ul><li>Semi-active Engine Mount</li><li>Active Engine Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive active engine mount market primarily encompasses two types: semi-active engine mounts and active engine mounts. Semi-active engine mounts adjust their stiffness based on real-time information about engine vibrations, enhancing ride comfort and noise reduction while maintaining some passive characteristics. In contrast, active engine mounts utilize advanced sensors and control systems to dynamically alter their properties in response to engine movement, providing superior vibration isolation and improved vehicle performance. Both types aim to enhance driving experience and reduce noise levels.</p></p>
