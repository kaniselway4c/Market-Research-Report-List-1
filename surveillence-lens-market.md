<p><p>The surveillance lens market primarily features two main types: C-Mount and CS-Mount lenses. C-Mount lenses have a flange back distance of 17.526 mm, allowing for greater versatility in lens attachment, making them suitable for various professional applications. In contrast, CS-Mount lenses have a shorter flange back distance of 12.5 mm, offering a more compact design ideal for smaller cameras. Both types cater to different surveillance needs, impacting resolution, field of view, and overall image quality in security systems.</p></p>
