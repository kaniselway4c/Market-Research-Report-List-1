<p><p>The floor insulation material market comprises various types, each offering unique benefits. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) are lightweight materials known for their moisture resistance. Rockwool provides excellent thermal insulation and fire resistance. PIR (Polyisocyanurate) and PU (Polyurethane) offer high insulation values with low thickness, making them energy-efficient choices. Glasswool is made from recycled glass, providing sound and thermal insulation, while "Others" include various innovative materials. Each type is selected based on specific insulation needs and application environments.</p></p>
