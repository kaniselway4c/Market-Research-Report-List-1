<p><p>Carbon nanotubes (CNTs) serve as advanced transparent conductors, segmented into three main types: Single-Walled Nanotubes (SWNTs), Double-Walled Nanotubes (DWNTs), and Multi-Walled Nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs exhibit exceptional electrical conductivity and flexibility, making them ideal for high-performance applications. DWNTs provide improved stability and conductivity compared to SWNTs. MWNTs offer greater mechanical strength and are cost-effective for large-scale applications. These properties position CNTs as promising materials for use in flexible displays, photovoltaics, and touch screens.</p></p>
