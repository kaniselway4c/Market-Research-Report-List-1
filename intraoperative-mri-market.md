<p><p>Intraoperative MRI (iMRI) technology is categorized based on magnetic field strength, primarily 0.2T, 1.5T, and 3.0T. The 0.2T systems are typically more portable and cost-effective, suitable for neurosurgical procedures with less stringent imaging requirements. The 1.5T systems offer a balance between image quality and operational efficiency, making them widely adopted for various surgeries.
