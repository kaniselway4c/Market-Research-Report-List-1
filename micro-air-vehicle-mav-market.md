<p><p>Micro Air Vehicles (MAVs) are small, unmanned aerial systems designed for various applications, including surveillance, reconnaissance, and research. The MAV market can be categorized into two main types: fixed-wing and rotary-wing. Fixed-wing MAVs feature a rigid structure with wings and offer efficient flight over longer distances, ideal for extensive coverage. In contrast, rotary-wing MAVs, such as drones, utilize rotating blades for vertical take-off and landing, providing enhanced maneuverability and the ability to operate in confined spaces. Both types serve diverse operational needs in military, commercial, and scientific sectors.</p></p>
