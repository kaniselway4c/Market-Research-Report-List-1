<p><p>OSB sheathing, or oriented strand board, is categorized into four main types based on its load-bearing capabilities and applications. OSB/1 is for interior use in dry conditions, while OSB/2 is suitable for non-structural applications in dry environments. OSB/3 is designed for load-bearing use in humid conditions, making it ideal for structural applications. OSB/4 is the strongest, intended for high-load-bearing applications in humid environments. Each type caters to specific building needs, ensuring safety and durability.</p></p>
