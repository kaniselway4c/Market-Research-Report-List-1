<p><ul><li>Drum Pre-Cleaner</li><li>Vibrating Mesh Pre-Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pre-cleaner market is categorized into two main types: drum pre-cleaners and vibrating mesh pre-cleaners. Drum pre-cleaners utilize rotating cylindrical screens to remove large debris and impurities from bulk materials, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, vibrating mesh pre-cleaners employ a series of vibrating screens with fine mesh to separate smaller particles and contaminants. Both types enhance material quality and processing efficiency, serving industries such as agriculture, mining, and food processing.</p></p>
