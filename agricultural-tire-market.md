<p><ul><li>Radial Agriculture Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Agriculture Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial and bias (crossply) tires. Radial agriculture tires feature steel belts that allow for better flexibility and improved traction, enhancing fuel efficiency and overall performance. In contrast, bias tires have layers arranged diagonally, offering sturdiness and a lower initial cost, making them suitable for various farming applications. Each type caters to different farming needs, with radial tires often used for high-speed operations and bias tires preferred for heavy-duty tasks.</p></p>
