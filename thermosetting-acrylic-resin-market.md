Acrylates are characterized by their flexibility, resistance to weathering, and high gloss finish, making them ideal for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, methacrylates offer superior thermal stability, strength, and clarity, often used in applications like automotive and construction materials. Both types contribute to a wide range of industries, addressing specific performance needs through their unique chemical properties and applications.</p></p>
