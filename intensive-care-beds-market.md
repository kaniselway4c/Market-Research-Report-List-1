<p><p>The Intensive Care Beds market comprises three main types: Manual, Electric, and Hydraulic Intensive Care Beds. Manual Intensive Care Beds require caregivers to adjust positions manually, providing basic functionality at a lower cost. Electric Intensive Care Beds, on the other hand, offer motorized adjustments for height and positioning, enhancing patient comfort and caregiver efficiency. Hydraulic Intensive Care Beds utilize hydraulic systems for smooth, precise adjustments and are often preferred for their ease of use and stability, especially in critical care settings.</p></p>
