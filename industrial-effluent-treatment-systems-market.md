<p><p>The Industrial Effluent Treatment Systems market comprises three primary types: physical, chemical, and biological methods. Physical methods involve the separation of contaminants through processes like filtration and sedimentation, ensuring solid waste removal. Chemical methods employ reactions to neutralize pollutants, utilizing coagulants or flocculants to precipitate harmful substances. Biological methods leverage microorganisms to degrade organic pollutants, promoting natural breakdown processes. Each method offers distinct advantages and is selected based on effluent characteristics and regulatory requirements for sustainable environmental management.</p></p>
