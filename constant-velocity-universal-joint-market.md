<p><strong>The Constant Velocity Universal Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball</li><li>Fork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Constant Velocity Universal Joint (CV Joint) market can be categorized mainly into two types: Ball and Fork. Ball joints utilize a spherical design that allows for smooth movement at varying angles, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, Fork joints employ a more traditional structure with two forks that grip a central ball, providing stability and durability in lower-speed applications. Both types are essential for ensuring smooth power transmission in vehicles, aiding in the overall efficiency of drivetrains.</p></p>
