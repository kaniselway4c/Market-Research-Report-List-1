<p><p>The conduit raceway market is segmented into three primary types: metal conduit raceway, plastic conduit raceway, and mixed material duct raceway. Metal conduit raceways, typically made from materials like steel or aluminum, offer superior durability and protection against external elements, making them ideal for industrial applications. Plastic conduit raceways, made from materials such as PVC, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for residential uses. Mixed material duct raceways combine different materials to leverage the benefits of both, catering to diverse installation needs and environments.</p></p>
