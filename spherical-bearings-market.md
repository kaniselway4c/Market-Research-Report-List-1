<p><p>Spherical bearings are specialized components that allow for misalignment and accommodate angular movement. Spherical ball bearings feature a spherical outer surface and a ball inside, providing smooth rotation. Spherical roller bearings utilize cylindrical rollers for increased load capacity and stability. Spherical plain bearings consist of a spherical surface on one part and a sliding interface on another, suitable for oscillating movements. Spherical rod end bearings combine a bearing and a rod end for pivoting solutions in mechanical systems. Other market types may include specialized variations designed for specific applications.</p></p>
