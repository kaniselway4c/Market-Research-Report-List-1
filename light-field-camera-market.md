<p><ul><li>Standard Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Focused Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Coded Aperture Camera</li><li>Stereo With Plenoptic Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light field camera market comprises several types, each with unique features. Standard plenoptic cameras capture a wide range of light rays, allowing for post-capture focus adjustments. Focused plenoptic cameras enhance image sharpness by concentrating on specific depths, while coded aperture cameras use mask patterns to capture depth information with enhanced control over light. Stereo with plenoptic cameras combine multiple viewpoints to create 3D images, offering a comprehensive perspective. These innovations enable advanced imaging capabilities across various applications.</p></p>
