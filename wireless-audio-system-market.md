<p><p>The wireless audio system market is categorized into several types based on transmission technology. RF (Radio Frequency) systems use radio waves for long-range communication, ideal for home theater setups. IR (Infrared) systems utilize light waves, suitable for short-range applications like remote controls but may be obstructed by barriers. Bluetooth technology offers flexibility and ease of use, making it popular for personal devices. Additionally, other emerging technologies may include Wi-Fi audio and proprietary protocols, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
