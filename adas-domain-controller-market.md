<p><p>ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) domain controllers are categorized based on their automation levels. Below Level 2 controllers manage basic functions like adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assistance. Level 2 to Level 3 controllers enable more complex tasks, including partial automation where the driver can disengage but must be ready to take control. Level 3 and above controllers support higher automation levels, facilitating automated driving in specific scenarios, reducing driver input, and enhancing vehicle intelligence and safety.</p></p>
