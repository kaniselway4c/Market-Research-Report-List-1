<p><strong>The Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Band Wireless Routers</li><li>Dual Band Wireless Routers</li><li>Tri Band Wireless Routers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless router market is categorized into single band, dual band, and tri-band routers. Single band routers operate at 2.4 GHz, suitable for basic internet tasks but limited in speed and range. Dual band routers support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz, allowing for better performance and reduced interference, ideal for streaming and gaming. Tri-band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, optimizing traffic for multiple devices and enhancing overall network performance, particularly in densely populated environments.</p></p>
