<p><p>The process chillers market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for applications with limited water access and typically requiring less maintenance. In contrast, water-cooled chillers employ water as a cooling medium, offering higher efficiency and cooling capacity, ideal for large industrial setups. Both types cater to various industries, including manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, each optimizing thermal management for operational efficiency.</p></p>
