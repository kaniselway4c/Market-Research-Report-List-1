<p><p>The industrial safety footwear market primarily comprises shoes and boots designed to protect workers in various hazardous environments. Safety shoes are typically lighter and offer basic protection, featuring steel or composite toe caps, slip-resistant soles, and puncture-resistant insoles. In contrast, safety boots provide enhanced protection, often covering the ankle for improved support and stability, and may include waterproof materials and thermal insulation. Both types are essential for minimizing injury risks and ensuring worker safety across industries like construction, manufacturing, and warehousing.</p></p>
